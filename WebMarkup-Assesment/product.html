1 Global story

IoT has definitely changed our lives in many ways. We are seeing everything around us become more personalized and intelligent. AI goes further by enabling devices to transform the way we think about health and well-being with useful insights.

2 What problem does this innovation solve?

IoT is driving the development of wearable devices in the healthcare industry, now known as the Internet of Health Things (IoHT), where medical devices and applications are connected to healthcare IT systems. The Internet of Things for medical devices involves machine-to-machine (M2M) communication and connectivity to cloud platforms where data generated by devices is collected, stored, and analyzed.

3 How to use wearable technologies

AI wearables can support fitness in everyday training activities. Most fitness wearables help users track their activity. When the user walks her 12000 steps, the wearable device counts and displays the steps. However, the limitation of these wearables is that at some point people will not understand how their data is used. In addition to tracking data, AI-enabled wearables can define things like what users need to eat, how much they need to sleep, and how they need to exercise to improve their fitness. increase.

The future of wearable technology has successes and failures.
